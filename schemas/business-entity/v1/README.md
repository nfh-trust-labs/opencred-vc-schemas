# Business Entity Credential

**ID:** `business-entity/v1`
**Type:** `BusinessEntityCredential`
**Anchor standard:** GLEIF vLEI; ISO 17442 LEI; ISO 3166-1 alpha-2

## Description

W3C VC representation of a legal business entity, modeled on the GLEIF vLEI field shape but wrapped in a W3C VC 2.0 envelope (not ACDC/KERI).

## When to use

Use this credential when you need to issue a business entity credential as a W3C Verifiable Credential. The schema is authored by OpenCred and anchored on GLEIF vLEI; ISO 17442 LEI; ISO 3166-1 alpha-2.

## Schema URL

`https://raw.githubusercontent.com/nfh-trust-labs/opencred-vc-schemas/main/schemas/business-entity/v1/schema.json`
